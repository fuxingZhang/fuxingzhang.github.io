Welcome to the Restroom Navigator Technical Support Center! If you encounter any problems or have any questions, please refer to the Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) below or contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How do I navigate to the desired restroom location?

    You can click on the restroom location within the app. The app will draw a route based on your current position to reach the destination.

  • Why can't I find restrooms near me?

    Please check if your location services are enabled and ensure your device has a good GPS signal. Additionally, some areas may not yet be included in our database.

  • How do I report incorrect information?

    When you find incorrect information, please send us a detailed report via the developer's email. We will verify and fix the error as soon as possible.
