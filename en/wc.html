Restroom Navigator - Technical Support

Welcome to the Restroom Navigator's technical support center! If you encounter any issues or have any questions, please refer to the following Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ) or contact us directly.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How to navigate to the target Restroom location?

    You can click on the restroom location within the app. The app will draw a route, and based on the route and your current location, guide you to your destination.

  • Why can't I find restrooms near me?

    Please check if your location services are turned on and ensure that your device has a good GPS signal. Additionally, some areas may not yet be included in our database.

  • How to report BUG?

    When you discover BUG, please send us a detailed report via the developer's email. We will verify and fix the BUG as soon as possible.
